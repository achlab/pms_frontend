## â“ FAQs

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions sent to you.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: No, email addresses cannot be changed. Contact support if you need to update it.

**Q: How do I verify my email?**
A: Check your email for a verification link after registration. Click the link to verify.

### Payments

**Q: How do I pay my rent?**
A: Pay through your preferred method (mobile money, bank transfer, etc.), then record the payment in the Invoices section.

**Q: When is rent due?**
A: Check your lease details for the payment due day (usually shown on dashboard).

**Q: What if my payment is late?**
A: Late payments may incur penalties. Check your lease for late payment terms. Pay as soon as possible.

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**
A: This depends on your lease agreement. Contact your landlord to discuss payment arrangements.

### Maintenance

**Q: How long does it take to fix maintenance issues?**
A: Response times vary by priority. Emergency issues are handled immediately, while normal issues typically take 24-48 hours.

**Q: Can I track my maintenance request?**
A: Yes! View all your requests in the Maintenance section and see real-time status updates.

**Q: What if my request is urgent?**
A: Set the priority to "Urgent" or "Emergency" when creating the request. Emergency issues are prioritized.

**Q: Can I cancel a maintenance request?**
A: Contact your property manager or caretaker to cancel a request.

### Lease

**Q: When does my lease expire?**
A: Check the "My Lease" page or dashboard for your lease end date.

**Q: How do I renew my lease?**
A: Contact your landlord before the lease expires to discuss renewal options.

**Q: Can I terminate my lease early?**
A: Check your lease agreement for termination terms. Usually requires notice (e.g., 30 days).

### Technical Issues

**Q: The website isn't loading. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser.

**Q: I'm getting an error message. What does it mean?**
A: Error messages usually explain the issue. Try the suggested action or contact support.

**Q: Can I use the system on my phone?**
A: Yes! The system is fully responsive and works on all devices.
